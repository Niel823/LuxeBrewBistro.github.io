    About us

    Founded in 2024 by Niel Alumbro, LuxeBrew Bistro began with a vision to bring people together through the art of coffee and comfort. What started as a small café in Parañaque soon became a cozy haven for those seeking great brews, good food, and genuine connection.

    Rooted in passion and creativity, LuxeBrew Bistro continues to craft every cup with care and purpose. From our signature blends to our freshly made meals, we aim to serve experiences that inspire warmth, relaxation, and a taste of everyday luxury.
